About the Author



Ives was born on March 6, 1940, and grew up in New England, California, and the Midwest. A reclusive and seldom seen author best known for his 1970 novel Monster Cottage Kill, Ives remains a shadowy figure on the fringes of literature. Little is known about his present whereabouts or whether he is still actively writing, although rumors abound.

More information about Ives can be found at the Edward Ives Home Page.
